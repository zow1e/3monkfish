# Product Brief: PetCare Copilot

PetCare Copilot helps pet owners track pet care records and interact with an AI assistant that provides safety-oriented guidance with citations.

## Core capability areas

- Profile and records management
- Appointment and reminder orchestration
- Concern tag extraction/suggestion
- FAQ and knowledge retrieval
- Listings discovery for providers/services/products
- Safety-aware response shaping and escalation hints
